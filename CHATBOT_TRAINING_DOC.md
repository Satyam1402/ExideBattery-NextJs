## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Battery Selection
**Q: How do I choose the right battery for my car?**
A: Check your vehicle manual for battery specifications (Ah capacity, dimensions). Our automotive batteries range from 35Ah to 100Ah. The Exide FMI0-MRED45 (45Ah) fits most standard cars.

**Q: What's the difference between tubular and flat plate batteries?**
A: Tubular batteries (like IT500) offer longer life and better deep discharge performance, ideal for inverters. Flat plate batteries are suitable for automotive applications with frequent charging.

### Warranty & Service
**Q: What does the warranty cover?**
A: Warranty covers manufacturing defects, premature failure, and performance issues. Does not cover physical damage, overcharging, or misuse.

**Q: Do you provide installation service?**
A: Yes, free installation is included with every battery purchase in Delhi NCR. Our certified technicians will install and test the battery at your location.

### Pricing & Payment
**Q: Are your prices negotiable?**
A: Our prices are competitive and include installation. We offer discounts on bulk orders and have EMI options for purchases above ₹5,000.

**Q: Do you accept old battery exchange?**
A: Yes, we accept old batteries and provide exchange value. Old battery disposal is handled responsibly following environmental guidelines.

### Delivery & Service Area
**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We serve entire Delhi NCR including Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. Same-day delivery available for most areas.

**Q: How quickly can you deliver and install?**
A: Same-day delivery and installation available for orders placed before 2 PM. Emergency service available 24/7 for critical applications.
